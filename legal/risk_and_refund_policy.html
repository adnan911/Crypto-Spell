⚠️ NFT Minting Risk Disclosure

By using NFT minting or blockchain features in CryptoSpell, you acknowledge the following risks:

1. Blockchain Transactions Are Final

Blockchain transactions cannot be reversed or modified once confirmed on the Solana network.

2. Network & Protocol Fees

Transactions require network fees (SOL) and protocol fees. These are determined by the blockchain network and are subject to change based on congestion.

3. Transaction Failures

Transactions may fail due to network congestion, wallet connectivity issues, or insufficient SOL balance. Network fees paid for failed transactions are generally not recoverable.
